Teaching

Traitement du Signal (Signal Processing)

Institution: École Centrale de Lille

Volume: 34 hours per year

Years: 2023-2024, 2024-2025

Teaching fundamental concepts in signal processing including sampling theory, Fourier analysis, filtering techniques, and digital signal processing applications.

Représentation du Signal et Problèmes Inverses (Signal Representation and Inverse Problems)

Institution: École Centrale de Lille

Volume: 30 hours per year

Years: 2023-2024, 2024-2025

Introduction to signal representation methods and inverse problem solving techniques, covering topics such as sparse representations, regularization methods, and optimization algorithms for signal reconstruction.
